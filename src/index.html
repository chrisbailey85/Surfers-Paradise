About us

Jacob Jones Came down to Cornwall in the early 2000s on holiday and fell in love with Fistral beach. Fistral beach is one of the world's top surfing destinations. All the big UK competitions take place here including the Famous Night Surf, Boardmaster's Surf Championships, National Surf Championships, and many more. Jacob has loved surfing all his life the only thing he loves more than surfing is teaching people how to surf. At Surfers Paradise you will find everything you need. Whether you are a beginner or experienced we can help you with all your Surfer needs. We look forward to seeing you.
